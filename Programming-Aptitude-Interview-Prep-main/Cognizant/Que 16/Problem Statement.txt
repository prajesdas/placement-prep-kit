Ananya Pandey’s professor has suggested her to prepare well for the lesson on seasons. While her professor says a month, she needs to tell the season corresponding to that month. Write a program to solve the above task.

Spring – March to May,
Summer – June to August,
Autumn – September to November and,
Winter – December to February.
The month should be in the range of 1 to 12. If not the output should be “Invalid month”.
Sample Input 1:

Enter the month: 08
Sample Output 1:

Season: Summer
Sample Input 2:

Enter the month: 14
Sample Output 2:

Invalid month
