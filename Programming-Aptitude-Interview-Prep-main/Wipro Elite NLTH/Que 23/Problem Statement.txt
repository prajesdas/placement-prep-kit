Problem: you are given a number, and you have to extract the key by finding the difference between the sum of the even and odd numbers of the input.

Test Case :

Input : 24319587

Output :  11

Explanation : odd terms : 3 + 1 + 9 + 5 + 7 = 25

                              even terms : 2 + 4 + 8 = 14

                              output : 11 (25-14)
