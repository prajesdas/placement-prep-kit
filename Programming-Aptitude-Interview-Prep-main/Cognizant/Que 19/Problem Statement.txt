Bela teaches his son to find the factors of the given numbers. When he provides a number to his son, he should tell the factors of that number. Help him to do this, by writing a program. 

Note:

If the input is zero (0) then the output will be "No Factors".
If the input is any negative number, then first convert it positive solve.
Sample Input 1:

54

Sample Output 1:

Factors for 56 are:

1

2

4

7

8

14

28

56

 

Sample Input 2:

-256

Sample Output 2:

Factors of 256 are:

1

2

4

8

16

32

64

128

256
